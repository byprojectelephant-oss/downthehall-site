More resources coming soon — Motor & Movement and more

Made by people who are always just down the hall.

Good resources for individuals with developmental disabilities are hard to find and even harder to make from scratch. Day program staff, group home workers, and community center volunteers spend hours searching for activities that actually work — time that could be spent with the people they support.

Down the Hall exists to fix that. Everything here is free to download, print, and use — no account, no paywall, no strings. Just practical worksheets and activities designed for the settings where this work actually happens.

We're just getting started. If you work in this space and have resources to share, or feedback on what we've made, we'd love to hear from you.

Free at the core

Hundreds of worksheets are always free — no account needed, no strings attached.

Built for the setting

Designed for day programs and group homes — not classrooms or clinics.

No jargon

Plain language instructions so anyone can pick up and use a resource.

Community-driven

Shaped by feedback from the people who use these resources every day.

Support Down the Hall Resources

Every worksheet on this site is created to give staff, caregivers, families, and community programs practical resources they can use right away. A voluntary contribution helps make more free resources possible.
