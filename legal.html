Privacy Policy

What we collect and why

Last updated: June 2026

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit this site and what we do with it.

Information you give us

If you fill out the contact form on this site, we collect the information you enter — your name, email address, role, and message. We use this only to respond to your message. We do not add you to any mailing list or share your information with third parties.

Information collected automatically

Like most websites, basic technical information may be logged when you visit — such as your browser type and general location (country or region). This is handled by our hosting provider (Netlify) and is used only for site performance and security. We do not use this to identify you personally.

Cookies & advertising

This site shows ads served by Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies and similar technologies (such as web beacons) to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. This may include personalized ads based on your browsing activity, or non-personalized ads if you have not consented to personalization.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings, or by visiting www.aboutads.info to opt out of third-party vendor cookies generally. Netlify, our hosting provider, may also set basic functional cookies required to serve the site — these do not track your browsing behavior.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how people use this site — for example, which worksheets are downloaded most often, which categories are browsed, and how the worksheet generators are used. Google Analytics may use cookies to collect information such as your approximate location (country or region), device and browser type, and the pages and resources you interact with. This information is aggregated and used only to help us improve the site and decide what resources to create next — we do not use it to identify you personally.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking across all websites by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Donations

If you choose to support this project, the "Donate" button on our Support page takes you to Ko-fi, a separate website where you can choose to make a voluntary contribution. We do not collect, see, or store any payment information — Ko-fi and its payment processors (such as PayPal) handle this directly under their own privacy policies. We only see that a donation was made, not your personal or payment details.

Third-party services

  • Netlify — our hosting provider. See Netlify's privacy policy
  • Google Fonts — used to display typography. Google may log font requests. See Google's privacy policy
  • Google AdSense — used to display advertising that helps keep this site free. Google may collect and use data to personalize ads and measure their performance. See how Google uses information from sites that use its services and Google's privacy policy
  • Google Analytics — used to measure site usage (downloads, page visits, generator use). See Google's privacy policy
  • Ko-fi — used to process optional donations. See Ko-fi's privacy policy

Children's privacy

This site is intended for use by staff, caregivers, and educators — not directly by children. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

Your rights

If you've submitted information through our contact form and would like it deleted, email us at hello@downthehallresources.com and we will remove it promptly.

Short version: We only collect what you give us through the contact form and never sell your data.
